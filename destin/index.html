Do Not Speak Aloud

Do not read these words lightly for they are the key to unlocking ancient settlement.

oak-beech-woodpecker + mushroom-webs-fawn + eldritch-deer-shell + red hawk-moss-tree + forest-fairy-ledge + cacaw.....

Today we stumbled upon a decrepit settlement. The once lush wooden cottages are now nothing more than husks. It is speculated that this area is an after image of the ancient Settlement of Schenley.

The collection of words on the left are remnants of an old eldritch spell. Though most consider it superstition, there are many that still practice the forbidden arts.

Unfortunately, when we tried to take pictures, the settlement would not show up in the photos. It was as if it fused with the surrounding foliage. Perhaps as a way to prevent the spread of its location. Oddly enough, none of us are able to recall where on the path we encountered it.
